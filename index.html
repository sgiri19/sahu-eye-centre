Dr. Purna Chandra Sahu (MS, Eye Specialist, Ex-CDMO, Sambalpur) is a distinguished ophthalmologist with extensive experience in eye care and vision health. Having served as the Chief District Medical Officer (CDMO), Dr. Sahu brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to his practice.

Frequently Asked Questions

What eye conditions do you treat?

We treat a wide range of eye conditions including refractive errors, cataracts, glaucoma, diabetic retinopathy, dry eyes, red eyes, and other common eye problems. We also provide comprehensive eye examinations and vision testing.

Do you provide spectacles and contact lenses?

Yes, we offer a wide range of spectacles and contact lenses at affordable prices. We have quality eyewear for all ages, and our team will help you choose the best option for your needs.

What are your consultation hours?

We are open Monday to Sunday from 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM and 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM. You can call us to book an appointment or walk in during these hours.

Do I need an appointment?

While appointments are recommended for better service, we also accept walk-in patients during our consultation hours. Calling ahead helps us serve you better.

Is Dr. P. C. Sahu available at other clinics?

No, Dr. P. C. Sahu only consults at Sahu Eye Centre & Optical, located at Infront of Chhabi Hotel, Bijaya Talkies Rd, Sundargarh. Please beware of fraud and only visit our official clinic.

What makes your clinic different?

Dr. Purna Chandra Sahu's extensive experience as Ex-CDMO and MS in Ophthalmology, combined with personalized patient care, modern equipment, and affordable pricing, makes us a trusted choice for eye care in Sundargarh.
